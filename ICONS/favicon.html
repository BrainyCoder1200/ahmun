What is AHMUN ?

AHMUN is the one of many events in our eminent institution that can boast of a platform that so deeply connects, contrasts and conceives the most unique solutions toward global issues.

It gives complete freedom to the students to let their creative ideas flow and effective imagination blossom, to solve global problems that deprive the world of love, peace and unity.

A world full of exciting new possibilities to ensure the successful handover of control to the New Generation, letting the future leaders and icons to truly realize their potential and its ideal discretion.

We are the World.

We are one Family.

We are AHMUN.

Welcome to our World.

Letter from the Secretary-General

Kashvi Jhawar

Dear Delegates and Executive Board Members, 

On behalf of the organising team, it is my honour to invite you to the Sixth Annual Conference of the Avalon Heights Model United Nations. This year, the event will be held on the 27th and 28th July. 


Over the years, AHMUN has continued to exemplify what it has always stood for - being a unique opportunity for students to hone their diplomatic skills in a neutral environment and aspire, attempt and achieve to become greater individuals. It is an environment that we have strived to create for the sixth year in a row. The premise of this event stems from the belief that students can prove themselves to be capable of taking the reins of the world, given the chance and freedom to prove it.

In today’s world, rapid advancements bring both incredible benefits and unexpected challenges. This is the paradox of progress. While technology and innovation improve our lives, they also create new problems like inequality and environmental issues. As future leaders, it's our task to address these complexities. This AHMUN, let's explore solutions to ensure progress that benefits everyone.


On my part, the initial experience as a delegate to being promoted to a Secretary-General over the years shaped me to present this event to you. I can only express the beauty of the event by sharing it with you, which is precisely the matter of what inspires us to make AHMUN all the more unique for you.


Please feel free to contact me or any other Secretariat member with any questions or concerns; or you can reach out to us at ahmun@avalonheights.co.in. We wish to see you all for AHMUN 2024!

Sincerely,

Kashvi Jhawar,

Secretary-General of AHMUN 6.0
